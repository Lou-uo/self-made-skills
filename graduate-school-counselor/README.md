# Graduate School Counselor Skill

A comprehensive skill to help undergraduate students navigate their graduate school application journey with personalized recommendations and guidance.

## 🎯 Overview

The Graduate School Counselor skill assists students with:
- **Personalized School Matching**: Recommend suitable graduate programs based on academic profile
- **Research Group Discovery**: Find relevant labs and professors aligned with research interests
- **Experience Analysis**: Search and analyze successful application experiences
- **Timeline Management**: Track deadlines, summer camps, and application milestones
- **Profile Optimization**: Manage and improve application materials

## 🚀 Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Profile
Tell the skill about your background:
```
I'm a Computer Science major at XYZ University with a 3.8 GPA. 
I have research experience in machine learning and participated in ACM-ICPC. 
I'm interested in AI/ML PhD programs.
```

### 2. Get School Recommendations
Ask for personalized suggestions:
```
Based on my profile, what graduate schools should I consider for AI research?
```

### 3. Find Research Groups
Discover specific labs and professors:
```
Show me machine learning research groups at top universities
```

### 4. Track Important Dates
Monitor application deadlines:
```
What are the upcoming deadlines for CS PhD programs?
Track MIT CSAIL for PhD applications
```

## 📊 Profile Components

### Academic Background
- Major, university, GPA, class rank
- Relevant coursework and specializations
- Research interests and career goals

### Experience Portfolio
- Publications and conference presentations
- Research projects and internships
- Competition awards and achievements
- Technical skills and certifications

### Test Scores
- TOEFL/IELTS scores
- GRE general and subject tests
- Score reporting plans

### Target Programs
- PhD and Master's program preferences
- Research area alignment
- Professor and lab preferences
- Safety/Match/Reach school categorization

## 🔍 Information Sources

The skill gathers information from:
- University official websites and program pages
- Graduate school forums (Reddit, The GradCafe, etc.)
- Application experience sharing platforms
- Academic ranking systems (QS, US News, etc.)
- Alumni networks and department statistics

## 📅 Application Timeline Management

### Summer Camps & Programs
- Research experience programs
- Summer schools and workshops
- Application deadlines and requirements

### Application Cycle
- Document preparation timeline
- Recommendation letter coordination
- Interview preparation schedule
- Decision deadline tracking
